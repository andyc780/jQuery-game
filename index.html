Welcome to Blitz Clicker!

This is a game all about reaction and clicking the correct squares quickly and accurately!

Note: Positive squares that fade out before being clicked counts as a miss and -3 HP and reduced accuracy.

The "?" button on the bottom right opens something interesting... I wonder will it will be?


Basic Tile Information

  • Light Blue - +1 Point, +0.5 HP
  • Light Green - +2 Points, +0.5 HP
  • Dark Green - +5 Points, + 0.5 HP
  • Gold - +8 Points, +1 HP
  • Light Red - -3 Points, -5 HP
  • Dark Red - -5 Points, -7 HP
  • Purple - -10 Points, -10 HP
  • Random (-15 to +15 Points), (-5 to +2) HP
  • Orange - Random Effect

Effect Information

  • Speed Up: Increases game speed by 1s
  • Regeneration: +1 HP/Second
  • Glass Cannon: x2 Points, Double the damage
  • Shrink: Reduced Board Size
  • Increase Points: +3 Points per positive box
  • Haze: Reduced Board Opacity
  • Shield: You cannot lose HP
